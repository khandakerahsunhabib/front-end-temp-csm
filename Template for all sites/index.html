FAQ

Is the trial free?
Yes, the trial of IntelliJ IDEA is free. You have access to a fully featured product for 30 days.
I want to try out JetBrains products in my company, but I cannot use the internet from our corporate network (a secure environment is used, products are evaluated offline, etc.). How can I do this?
Please contact our support: idea-support@jetbrains.com
How can I make my trial more effective?
Please subscribe to the educational emails, which will help you get the most out of the trial. You can subscribe right inside the product on the first install and startup. In these emails we share product tips, from basic advice for a quick start to more advanced topics. You can unsubscribe from them at any time.
