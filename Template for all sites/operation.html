Types of Roof Materials We Work with

The locally owned and operated company works with almost all types of roofing materials including

Metal Roofing: The materials used for this type are aluminum, copper, or steel. It’s durable and requires less maintenance. That’s why metal roofs are getting popular. A perfect choice for harsh weather conditions but can be noisy during natural events like rain and hailstorms.

Asphalt Shingles: Easier to install, affordable, environment-friendly, variety of styles, etc. are the advantages of asphalt shingles. It’s not as sustainable as metal roofing. Like metal roofing, it’s also a good fire resistant.

Roll Roofing: Roll roofing is preferable for unoccupied structures such as sheds, garages outbuildings, and other similar places. It’s easy to install and cost-effective.

Slate Shingles Roof: It’s among the roofing material types that are the oldest ones. Slate tiles are waterproof, durable, and insect-proof. They are quite expensive.
