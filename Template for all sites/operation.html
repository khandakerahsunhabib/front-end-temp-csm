How Often to Service a Heat Pump?

Your heat pump needs a professional technician service at least once a year. If your heat pump is the primary heating or cooling system of your home not complimentary then you need heat pump service twice a year by a professional technician.

Importance of Regular Servicing

Heat pump maintenance can get hard sometimes. It is important to service your heat pump to have a comfortable life. You need to service your heat pump for the mentioned advantages.

  • Increase Efficiency
  • Increase Durability
  • Improves Air Quality
  • Prevents Hazards

Annual Servicing vs. Emergency Servicing

Annual Servicing:

It's a good practice to have a professional technician heat pump service at least once a year. Ideally, it's better to schedule your service in the spring or fall when the system is not under heavy load.

Emergency Servicing:

When your heat pump doesn't serve its actual purpose or it doesn't work properly you need an emergency service. If you face the following signs then you need emergency heat pump servicing.

  • Reduction in Heating or Cooling
  • Unusual Noises
  • Ice Build-Up Increased
  • Energy Bills Frequent Cycling

Act Now for the Best Servicing

A regular servicing schedule is needed to keep your heat pump operating efficiently and effectively. By taking good care of your heat pump, you'll enjoy a comfortable indoor environment. Also, saves money on energy bills and avoids costly repairs in the long run.
