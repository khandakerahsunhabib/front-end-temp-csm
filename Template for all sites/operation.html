Rug Care Tips for Your Rug

You don’t have to do much to keep your rug clean and fresh. After getting professional rug cleaning, try to maintain a few things that will help you to have a fresh rug. Like,

  • Try to vacuum your rug periodically.
  • Avoid using any harsh cleaning solution or technique.
  • Avoid using a hard brush to clean your rug.
  • Rotate your rug to minimize discoloration, wear & tear.
  • Bolt your rug to clean accidental spills.
  • Last but not least, avoid using bleach or vinegar on the rug.
