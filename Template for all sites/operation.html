Step-by-Step Rug Cleaning Process by Ashly Rug Cleaners

To ensure a complete rug cleaning, we are here for you:

Step 1
Inspection & Pickup

Our rug cleaning professionals begin by inspecting the rug to assess its condition and identify any specific cleaning requirements. Once assessed, the rug is carefully picked up for transport to the cleaning facility.

Step 2
Follow-Up Inspection

After the initial inspection, we conduct a follow-up inspection to reevaluate the rug's condition and determine the appropriate cleaning methods.

Step 3
Dusting

Your rug undergoes a dusting process to remove dry soil and debris thoroughly. This step is crucial for preparing the rug for deeper cleaning.

Step 4
Clean, Wash & Rinse

Our expert cleaning involves a thorough wash and rinse process using specialized solutions to remove stains, dirt, and contaminants from the rug's fibers.

Step 5
Drying

Following the cleaning process, the rug is carefully dried to ensure it is moisture-free and to prevent any potential issues like mold or mildew.

Step 6
Combing & Brushing

Our technicians will comb and brush the rug to restore its natural texture and appearance.

Step 7
Fringe Grooming

We give attention to grooming and cleaning the fringes, ensuring they are tidy and well-maintained.

Step 9
Vacuuming & Final Inspection

A thorough vacuum is performed to remove any remaining particles. A final inspection by our team to ensure the rug meets quality standards.

Step 10
Delivery

Once the cleaning and maintenance processes are completed, the rug is delivered back to the customer in its refreshed and rejuvenated state.
